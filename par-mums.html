Slow, intentional objects made by hand — built to last for generations.

Atelier Minimal was founded with a simple belief: less is more, but only when every detail is considered.

We create small batches of functional objects — ceramics and wood pieces — using traditional techniques combined with a modern, restrained aesthetic. Each item is made to order, allowing us to focus on quality rather than quantity.

Our Approach

Everything we make is designed to age beautifully. We choose natural materials that develop character over time: stoneware clay that gains depth with use, solid walnut that darkens and reveals its grain. No trends, no shortcuts — just honest craftsmanship.

Slow Making

We work at a human pace. Every piece is thrown, shaped, or carved by hand in our small studio.

Thoughtful Materials

Only the highest quality clay, glazes, and sustainably sourced hardwoods. Nothing unnecessary.

Made to Order

When you order, the piece is created specifically for you. This reduces waste and ensures true individuality.

Behind the Objects

We are a small team of makers who find joy in the quiet repetition of craft. Our work is rooted in respect for material, function, and the people who will live with these objects every day.

Thank you for supporting slow, considered making.
